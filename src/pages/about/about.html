Discover FDL: your trusted partner in global logistics solutions

We offer integrated sea, air, inland, and warehouse distribution services, including door-to-door supply chain solutions

NINGBO FENDI INTERNATIONAL LOGISTICS CO., LTD (FDL), established in 2009, is a premier logistics company specializing in sea, air, and inland transportation. With branches strategically located in Shenzhen, Shanghai, and Qingdao, we offer comprehensive logistics solutions, including door-to-door supply chain services, supported by our direct booking rights and an extensive global agent network.

Our expertise spans key international markets such as Russia, the Middle East, the USA, and Asia. At FDL, we are committed to delivering unparalleled service, building trust with our clients, and continuously innovating to meet the evolving demands of modern supply chains. Our mission is rooted in providing exceptional customer experiences, cultivating long-term loyalty through sincerity, and driving growth through forward-thinking logistics solutions.

Our partners

We provide a full range of services, including space booking, customs clearance, sea freight, inland transportation, storage, and certification, all managed with professionalism and efficiency. Our real-time cargo tracking and timely updates help streamline operations, cut costs, and enhance productivity.
