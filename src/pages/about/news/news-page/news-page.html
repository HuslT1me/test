Russia-China Trade Turnover: Annual Results 2024

Let’s review the figures for January-December 2024:

According to data from the General Administration of Customs of China, trade turnover for the 12 months of 2024 increased by 1.9% compared to the same period in 2023, reaching $244.81 billion

  • Exports from China to Russia grew by 4.1% to $115.49 billion.
  • Imports of Russian goods remained unchanged at $129.32 billion.
  • Russia’s trade surplus with China decreased by 23.8% compared to the previous year, falling to $13.83 billion.

Although trade turnover between the two countries continues to grow, the pace is not as “explosive” as in the previous two years: +29.3% in 2022 ($190 billion) and +26.3% in 2023 ($240.11 billion).
